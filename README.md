## About Me

I am a graduate student in **Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems** at the **Shenyang Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (SIA, CAS)**, affiliated with the **University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS)**. I work in the institute's Intelligence Group under the supervision of **Prof. Zhi Han (韩志)**.

I received my bachelor's degree in **Robotics Engineering** from the **College of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, Hunan University**. My research interests lie at the intersection of robotics, multimodal foundation models, and efficient machine learning.

## Research Focus

| Area | Questions I care about |
| --- | --- |
| **Embodied Intelligence** | How can agents perceive, reason, plan, and adapt in open physical environments? |
| **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** | How can visual observations and language instructions be grounded into reliable robot actions? |
| **Vision-Language Navigation (VLN)** | How can robots follow long-horizon instructions using spatial reasoning, memory, and semantic mapping? |
| **World Models** | How can predictive representations and learned dynamics support planning, decision-making, and control? |
| **Efficient Foundation Models** | How can distillation, pruning, quantization, and efficient adaptation make large models practical on robots? |

## Current Research Agenda

I am currently building a systematic research foundation across:

- efficient and lightweight large models for embodied deployment;
- multimodal robot learning and general-purpose VLA policies;
- long-horizon VLN with memory and spatial reasoning;
- world models for prediction, planning, and decision-making;
- reproducible evaluation of embodied agents in realistic environments.

My near-term goal is to identify a meaningful research problem at the intersection of these areas, conduct rigorous and reproducible experiments, and develop the work into a high-quality paper.

## 中文简介

我目前就读于**中国科学院大学—中国科学院沈阳自动化研究所**，攻读**模式识别与智能系统**专业研究生，导师为**韩志老师**，所在团队为沈阳自动化所智能组。本科就读于**湖南大学人工智能与机器人学院机器人工程专业**。

我的研究愿景是推动机器人逐步具备接近人类的**感知、理解、推理、学习与行动能力**，使其能够适应复杂且陌生的物理环境，并帮助人们解决现实世界中的困难问题。

### 研究方向

- **具身智能**：研究智能体如何在开放物理环境中进行感知、推理、规划与自适应。
- **视觉—语言—动作模型（VLA）**：研究如何将视觉观测和语言指令可靠地映射为机器人动作。
- **视觉语言导航（VLN）**：关注长时序指令跟随、空间推理、记忆机制和语义地图。
- **世界模型**：利用预测表征和环境动力学支持机器人的规划、决策与控制。
- **大模型轻量化**：关注知识蒸馏、模型剪枝、量化、参数高效适配与机器人端侧部署。

### 当前计划

目前主要调研和复现**大模型轻量化、具身智能、世界模型、VLA 与 VLN**相关工作，并系统梳理重要方法、数据集、评测基准和开放问题。近期希望从这些方向的交叉点中找到具有实际价值的研究问题，开展严谨、可复现的实验，并形成一篇高水平论文。
